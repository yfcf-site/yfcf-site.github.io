Beta than Pong

Check out the Work in Progress area for information on the Beta than Pong project. Up to now, the Farmers have given two performances using the experimental Pong-setup: one at Logos in Ghent (03/95) and one at Steim in Amsterdam (04/95).

Sputnik: Internet goes Music

On May, 9th 1995, the Farmers surfed the Net before a live audience at the Beursschouwburg, a cultural center in Brussels. Check out their Sonic Bookmarks and the press release (in Dutch) for this so-called 'Sputnik' event organised by ISDM.
